About

First opened in 2005, Los Primos has been providing the Goshen area with great Mexican food for over a decade. Using only real ingredients and authentic recipes, it's no wonder our food is consistently voted the best Mexican in Elkhart county. Come have a traditional meal at our original, sit-down restaurant, or get your food made fast to order at our new location, opened in the summer of 2017.
